About me

My name is Aaron Winn. I live in West Jordan, Utah. I married my wife 6 years ago and we have 2 wonderful children a boy named Lucas 3 and little girl named Olivia 17 months. I am the oldest child of five. I have many hobbies I love doing that include wood working, fishing, hiking, ATV riding, snowboarding and shooting. We spend a lot of time outside and many of our weekends doing outdoor activities. Another hobby that I would wish to spend more time doing is photography. I love to take pictures but don't always take the time to do so. I have done many different things they include plumbing, electrical, quality control inspector, built gyroplanes a cross between a helicopter and airplane and installed home theater systems. Between all of the jobs I have had and my hobbies I have decided that I needed to do something different. Something that is not so physically demanding.
